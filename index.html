About Me

I am a graduate of the University of Florida in Gainesville with two degrees in Electrical Engineering and Computer Engineering. I have extensive background in wireless communications, and I have primarily worked for wireless telecom companies such as Motorola and Nokia. Due to the recent advances in mobile communications and introduction of the 5G standards, I think that mobile industry will experience slower growth in the coming years. As a result, I have decided to shift my focus to internet technologies and become a full-stack developer.

My favorite hobby is traveling, specially to Europe and the Caribbean. I hope someday I can own a small studio in Paris or Florence and spend extended periods of time in Europe. I also enjoy doing BBQ, doing small construction and remodeling projects, running, swimming, listening to music from around the world, and collecting wine.
